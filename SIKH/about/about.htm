About the Organization

  1. Kashmir Sikh Sahayak Sabha is the only social organization in the Kashmir valley.
  2. Founder of Kashmir Sikh Sahayak Sabha is Late Gunwant Singh . He was also the first President of the Sabha. He worked tirelessly, selflessly for the upliftment of the Sabha.
  3. Kashmir Sikh Sahayak Sabha was established in July 1970. It has completed 44 years in the service of widows, orphans, destitute and needy.
  4. Constitution of the Sabha was made effective on the 1st day of July, 1970 and is effective till date.
